About us

In 1270 Bengal, Govinda Roy Chowdhury made Roy houseGobindvbonin Dhamrai at Dhaka. Gobindvbon locate beside by gajikhali river. Gobindvbon is the earliest Zamindar house in Bangladesh. Now, this house age is 200 years. For 115 years there is Durga Puja which is the culture of the Roy house. Govinda Roy Chowdhury had six sons and one daughter. For this reason Govinda Roy Chowdhury made six building. All building have their own name. Still all descendants of Govinda Roy Chowdhury live their Gobindvbon.
